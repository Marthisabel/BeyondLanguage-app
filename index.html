learning rules


If you learn to correctly pronounce all the letters of the alphabet and memorize the 3 essential rules for placing the stress correctly, then you will quickly get a proper accent. All letters are pronounced except the "u" in the syllables "gue", "gui", "que" and "qui", unless this "u" has an umlaut, as in "shame". The "h" is also silent. The tonic accent is very important and the logic of its placement in the word is based on three essential rules, easy to memorize: When a word ends with a vowel, an "n" or an "s", the accent will be on the penultimate syllable.
